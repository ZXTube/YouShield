At YouShield, accessible from the Chrome Web Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by YouShield and how we use it.

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you. The following types of personal information may be collected, stored, and used:

The security of your personal information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
